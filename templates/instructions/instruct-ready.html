In this experiment, you will play the role of a research laboratory technician examining classifications of gemstones.

We will ask you to classify eight specimens.

You can make your judgments based on two sources of information. One source of information is the laboratory tests you run yourself. The second source of information is judgments from previous technicians.

The tests you run yourself have a certain degree of reliability. Each test produces evidence for the correct classification with probability 0.6, and produces evidence for incorrect classifications with probability 0.5.

The set of technicians working on this project (i.e., previous MTurk workers) operate in shifts. You will be able to observe the judgment of workers from the previous shift as long as you are not in the first shift.
